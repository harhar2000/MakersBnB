About MakersBnB

Welcome to MakersBnB, the destination for travellers seeking unique stays and memorable experiences.

At MakersBnB, we believe every journey is as individual as you. That’s why along with our high grade website, we curated a diverse collection of properties, each with its own character and charm.

How It Works:

  1. Sign Up & Log In: Creating an account. Our straightforward sign-up process ensures you’re on your way to discovering your next stay.
  2. Discover Unique Properties: Browse through our extensive list of spaces, where you’ll find detailed descriptions, authentic photographs, and transparent pricing.
  3. Making Requests: Once you’ve found your perfect space, head over to the requests page. Here, you can manage your stays.
  4. Listing Spaces: List your space with us and join our community of hosts.
  5. Manage Your Requests: As a host, go to the requests page to see who’s interested in staying at your space. Approve requests here.
